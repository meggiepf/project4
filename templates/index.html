Loan Prediction Payment Plan

The purpose of this project was to analyze a prediction of a candidates repayment plan on a loan based on interest rate, total payments, revolving utilization, inquiry in the last six months, and debt to income ratio.


Data and visualizations are are provided as part of the analysis, including explanations and descriptions of trends and correlations.
